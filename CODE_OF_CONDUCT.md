# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We, as contributors and maintainers of **Elo Terapêutico**, pledge to make participation in this project a harassment‑free experience for everyone, regardless of age, gender, sexual orientation, disability, physical appearance, body size, race, ethnicity, religion (or lack thereof), or any other protected characteristic.

## Our Standards

- **Be respectful.** Treat others with kindness and be mindful of tone.
- **No harassment.** Harassment includes profanity, insults, sexual or hateful comments, or any targeted attacks.
- **Stay on topic.** Keep discussions focused on the project and its goals.
- **Provide constructive feedback.** Critique ideas, not the person.
- **Report violations.** If you encounter behavior that violates this code, please contact a maintainer directly.

## Enforcement

Maintainers will review any reports and take appropriate actions, which may include a warning, temporary ban, or permanent removal from the project.
